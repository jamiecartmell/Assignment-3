Simply Chef, a global company founded by Jamie Cartmell, is on a mission to empower people like you to lead healthier, happier lives. Simply Chef - A Culinary Journey Guided by Passion and Care


Founded at the beginning of 2023, Simply Chef is the result of the relentless dedication and passion, Jamie Cartmell. My journey began with a deep-seated commitment to our customers and a shared sense of duty to make a difference in their lives.

I embarked on this adventure from a 'humble' start, where long nights and seven-day workweeks were our steadfast companions. This hands-on approach allowed us to remain intricately connected not only to our customers but also to our valued suppliers and the very heart of our operations.

At Simply Chef, we care deeply about the experiences you have with us. It's a commitment we take seriously, which is why we continually reinvest every ounce of our energy and resources into enhancing your journey with us. We believe that food is not just sustenance; it's a powerful lifestyle choice that has a profound impact on your well-being.

Today, Simply Chef has risen to become a global meal delivery service. As we pen this message, our offerings stand as a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and excellence. We boast the largest and most diverse menu, prioritizing local, top-quality ingredients. Our mission is to provide you with healthier options, unrivaled variety, unmatched flexibility, and seamless customer support that's just a message away.

Our core focus remains on our products, and we vow to deliver nothing short of first-class service. We welcome your feedback and are eager to hear from you. If there's anything we can do to elevate your experience, please reach out to us at simply@chef.ie.
